Cinnamon Rolls

Description:

Learn to bake the most delicious cinnamon rolls!

Ingredients

Hard to find ingredients!

  • 400g butter
  • 250g love
  • 1000g pure hugs
  • 2ml distilled happiness

Steps

Follow along!

  1. Dunk in the butter in a fancy fontainer
  2. Mix in the love, kindly
  3. Add the intense pure hugs
  4. Mix
  5. Finish it up by adding the distilled happiness
  6. Bake!
